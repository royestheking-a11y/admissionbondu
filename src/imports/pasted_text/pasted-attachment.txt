You can extend your BD admission‑support system with scholarship and quota filters by adding a few extra layers to your filters and database.

Below is a clear, practical design you can follow (scholarship + quota filters + DB structure + example filters).

1. Types of scholarships to support
Most private universities in Bangladesh use:

Merit‑based scholarships

Based on SSC/HSC/Diploma GPA (e.g., 5.00, 4.8, 4.5, etc.).

Ranges: 10%–100% tuition‑fee waiver.

Social/need‑based scholarships

For financially disadvantaged students, freedom‑fighter children, or indigenous‑minority students.

Often 50%–100% tuition‑fee waiver.

Renewable scholarships

Require minimum CGPA per year (e.g., 3.0, 3.3, 3.5) to keep.

Your system can let students select what kind of scholarship they qualify for (merit, need, FF, etc.).

2. Quota‑based filters
Common quotas in private‑university admissions in Bangladesh:

Freedom‑Fighter (FF) quota

Children of freedom fighters may get 50%–100% tuition‑fee waiver.
​

Indigenous/ethnic‑minority quota

Reserved seats or special waiver in some universities.
​

District/region‑based quota (rural / remote area).
​

Special‑talent / sports quota

Some universities give partial‑fee support for sports/cultural excellence.
​

You can add these as checkbox filters or dropdown filters in the UI.

3. How to design the filters in your system
You can show filters like:

Scholarship filters

“I want 100% tuition‑fee waiver”

“I want 50%‑75% waiver”

“I want 20%‑49% waiver”

“I want only merit‑based waiver”

“I want need‑based / FF quota”

Quota filters

“Freedom‑Fighter child quota”

“Indigenous / ethnic‑minority quota”

“Rural / remote‑district quota”

“Special‑talent (sports/art) quota”

GPA‑linked filters

“I have SSC/HSC GPA ≥ 5.0”

“I have SSC/HSC GPA 4.8–4.99”

“I have SSC/HSC GPA 4.5–4.79”

“I have SSC/HSC GPA < 4.5”

Based on this, the system shows only universities that match your scholarship/quota rule and GPA.

4. Database fields to add
To support these filters, extend your universities / programs / fees tables with:

Per‑university scholarship fields

merit_scholarship_eligible (bool)

merit_scholarship_min_gpa_ssc (e.g., 4.5)

merit_scholarship_min_gpa_hsc (e.g., 4.8)

merit_scholarship_min_gpa_diploma (if applicable)

merit_scholarship_percent_min / max (e.g., 10–100%)

Need‑based / quota fields

has_ff_quota (bool)

ff_quota_percent (e.g., 50–100%)

has_indigenous_quota (bool)

has_rural_quota (bool)

has_sports_talent_quota (bool)

Renewal conditions

scholarship_min_cgpa (e.g., 3.0, 3.3, 3.5)

Then, when a user selects:
